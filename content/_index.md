**PRISM** is an AI research lab at [UCL](http://www.ucl.ac.uk), led by
[Gabriel Brostow](http://www0.cs.ucl.ac.uk/staff/g.brostow/).


We work on blue-sky problems in AI, especially in Computer Vision and
Human–Computer Interaction (HCI). Most of our work sits under the umbrella
of Human-Centered AI — vision and AI systems that genuinely help people.
